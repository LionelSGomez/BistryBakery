All of our ingredients are organic. We use only the best quality flours that have been stone ground to preserve the wheat germ (which contains most of the vitamins and minerals). Only three ingredients to make our sourdough breads, we only use three ingredients: flour, filtered water and sea salt. No other additives, no preservatives, no sugar, no fat, no chemical yeast, no enzymes!

Our creative pastry chefs are constantly playing and experimenting with different ingredients, tastes, forms, and decorations in order to provide our customers with a unique cake eating experience. We are able to provide the best results by discussing expectations of cake presentation, taste testing the desserts and through our passion to fulfill the consumer's sweet dreams.

There are no words to describe our delicatessen. You have to taste them to understand it.
